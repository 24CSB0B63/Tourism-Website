Vidhana Soudha
The iconic seat of Karnataka’s legislature, Vidhana Soudha is a masterpiece of Neo-Dravidian architecture. Built in 1956, it features grand granite facades, intricate carvings, and a majestic central dome. Illuminated at night, it symbolizes the state’s governance and cultural heritage, standing as a testament to Bangalore’s architectural grandeur.
